# Lovelist Playbook

A curated resale operations playbook documenting the systems, strategies, and standards used to build a modern boutique resale business.

---

## Overview

Lovelist is a curated resale concept focused on elevated, everyday style without the retail markup. This repository serves as the operational backbone of the business, outlining how inventory is sourced, evaluated, priced, and sold.

The goal is to create a streamlined, repeatable system that balances quality, efficiency, and brand experience.

---

## What This Includes

- Sourcing and intake guidelines  
- Inventory evaluation and buying criteria  
- Pricing and markdown strategy  
- Content and sales approach  
- Operational workflows  

---

## Business Philosophy

This is not a traditional thrift or volume-based resale model.

Lovelist is built on:
- **Curation over quantity** — only items that meet quality and style standards are accepted  
- **Efficiency over complexity** — simple systems that are easy to execute consistently  
- **Experience over inventory** — presenting items in a way that makes them easy to buy  

---

## Key Principles

- Buy with intention, not emotion  
- Prioritize items that are easy to style and wear  
- Focus on sell-through speed over maximum margin  
- Continuously refine standards based on real performance
